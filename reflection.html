Gratitude Towards Simple Things in Life Exercise:

Objective: To cultivate appreciation for simple everyday things.

Instructions: Create a list of simple, everyday things that you are glad you have in your life. These could be sensory experiences, like a smell, taste, color, or sound, or even simple activities, like taking a walk and stuff.

Mindfulness Meditation

Objective: To practice mindfulness and appriciate the present

Instructions: Meditate for at least 10 minutes, focusing on your breath and any thoughts you have. This can be done in a quiet space, such as a calm and tranquil room. meditation is a great way to relax and de-stress.
